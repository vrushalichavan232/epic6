About Us

“We believe that every individual has potential in its own kind”.

Nature Lovers is non profitable non-government orginization which works with active participation of nearly 200 members. The working of the Team is divided into two parts i.e NATURE ENVIRONMENT PROGRAM (NEP) and COMMUNITY ACTION SERVICE(CAS).the idea behind working in two different sub-team is to have major impact on two differnt part of our society.

NATURE ENVIRONMENT PROGRAM (NEP)
the working of NEP mainly focuses on the young generation of our country.the NEP team come with differnt parameters and selected schools are targeted to complete this paramenters.with the help of this we give the opportunity to the theoritical knowledge into practical.the program starts in the month of june and ends in December.

COMMUNITY ACTION SERVICE(CAS)
The working of CAS is somewhat different.the cas team works with schools which are for specially abled.the team goes there and take different workshop for this student with an intention to motivate them. at same time some of the member delivers lecture to under priveledge students,teach them and motivate them. the program starts in the month of june and ends in December.
